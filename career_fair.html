KOLKATA CAREER FAIR 2018

Biggest career fair of the year now in kolkata

In association with   MYWBUT.COM – Home for Engineers of West Bengal

This year grab an opportunity like never before. Choose from over 300 well renowned institutions of higher education. Also more than 50 companies will also grace us with their presence at the event.
Over  20,000  students have already registered.

Timings   30th January’18, 11 am. onwards
  Venue    Informatica Park, Sector V Salt Lake, Kolkata – 91

The purpose of this career fair is to provide the students with the 'tools' and resources to make important and valuable career decisions. During the Career Fair, the students will take their 'first steps' towards a brighter future by meeting the representatives of various esteemed institutions face to face and exchange information.

Students will be informed about what they’ll need to know about career paths, employer expectations and professional opportunities. The will also get to know the differences between various courses and their respective merits and demerits.
